Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Kleo ("we", "our", "us") is a personal finance management application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address.
  • Financial Data: Bank account balances, transaction history, and account details accessed through Plaid.
  • User-Created Data: Goals, budgets, and manually entered transactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Display your financial accounts and transactions within the app
  • Provide budgeting, goal tracking, and financial insights
  • Improve our services and user experience

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Plaid: To securely connect your bank accounts. Plaid's privacy policy is available at plaid.com/legal.
  • Supabase: For secure data storage with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • Google OAuth: For authentication.

4. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest
  • Row-level security ensures each user can only access their own data
  • PIN and biometric authentication protect app access on each device
  • We never store your bank credentials — Plaid handles all bank connections securely

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with service providers (Plaid, Supabase) as necessary to operate the app.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon account deletion, all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke bank account access at any time through the app or Plaid portal

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
